# Corporate Financial Analysis Project

This project performs a comprehensive financial analysis of a corporate entity over multiple fiscal years (2020-2021). It utilizes advanced Excel modeling to evaluate financial stability, operational efficiency, and overall market performance.

## Key Project Stages
* **Data Consolidation:** Aggregating balance sheets, profit and loss statements, and cash flow data from multi-year financial reports.
* **Ratio Analysis:** Calculation and interpretation of key performance indicators (KPIs) across four main categories:
    * **Liquidity Analysis:** Assessment of the company's ability to meet short-term obligations.
    * **Debt & Solvency:** Evaluation of long-term financial structure and leverage.
    * **Efficiency (Activity) Ratios:** Analysis of asset management and operational turnover.
    * **Profitability Analysis:** Investigation of margins (ROE, ROA, ROS) and net earnings quality.
* **Vertical and Horizontal Analysis:** Examining trends over time and the structural composition of assets and liabilities.
* **Cash Flow Examination:** Detailed audit of operating, investing, and financing cash flows.

## Main Conclusions
* The comparative analysis between 2020 and 2021 revealed key trends in the company's capital structure and liquidity position.
* The utilization of profitability ratios provided insights into the efficiency of revenue generation relative to equity and total assets.
* The structured Excel model allows for rapid re-assessment and sensitivity analysis based on updated financial inputs.

## Technologies
* **Analysis Tool:** Microsoft Excel
* **Methods:** Ratio Analysis, Cash Flow Analysis, Vertical/Horizontal Financial Statement Analysis
* **Documentation:** PDF Financial Statements, Integrated Spreadsheet Modeling
